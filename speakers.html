Meet Our Speakers

Industry experts and tech leaders who inspired us students with their knowledge and real-world experience from leading companies.

Ms. Zoe Diaz De Rivera

IBPAP Representative

Ms. Zoe from IBPAP provided valuable insights into the IT-BPM industry, emphasizing its role as a major employment driver in the Philippines. She highlighted the importance of critical thinking, effective communication skills, and building strong digital portfolios to enhance career opportunities in the tech sector.

Atty. Yves Gonzalez

Head of Government Affairs & Public Policy

Atty. Gonzalez from Google Philippines shared insights on AI innovation and ethical technology practices. He emphasized Google's mission and demonstrated how passion combined with technical skills can create meaningful impact in the tech industry.

Ms. Beatrice Grace Bonjibod

Brand Marketing Specialist

Ms. Bonjibod from Teleperformance highlighted their leadership in digital business services and people-centered technology solutions. She emphasized the importance of empathy, collaboration, and communication skills alongside technical expertise, while noting their commitment to gender diversity with 53% female workforce.

Ralph Vincent Regalado

Chief Scientist and Head of AI

Mr. Ralph Vincent Regalado from Kollab introduced their mission of empowering digital innovation through collaborative technology solutions. He shared insights on their dynamic approach to AI development, software engineering, and human-centered design principles

Jazmine Calma

Senior Engineer for Quality Assurance

"Ms. Calma, Senior QA Engineer, delivered a compelling talk on workplace culture and its importance alongside technical skills. She emphasized the value of building not just what works, but how we work together, highlighting the significance of inclusive and healthy workplace environments.

Toni-Jan Keith Monserrat

Senior Principal Engineer for AI and Data

Mr. Toni-Jan Keith Monserrat, Senior Principal Engineer for AI and Data, presented on practical AI applications across various industries. He demonstrated how AI transforms real-world sectors like agriculture, customer service, and healthcare, bridging the gap between academic theory and practical implementation.

Miguel Siriban

UI/UX Designer and UI Developer

Mr. Siriban, UI/UX Designer and UI Developer at Kollab, delivered a practical session on building effective developer portfolios. He shared strategies for showcasing technical skills, telling compelling personal stories through design, and creating portfolios that stand out in today's competitive job market.

Ms. Heidee Enriquez

CEO of MicroSourcing and Beepo

Ms. Heidee, CEO of MicroSourcing and Beepo, emphasized the importance of adaptability, curiosity, and continuous learning in the rapidly evolving tech industry. Her inspiring message highlighted how academic studies connect to real-world industry opportunities and career growth.

Peter Fizelley

Chief Information Security Officer

Mr. Peter Fizelley, Chief Information Security Officer at ADB, shared valuable insights on cybersecurity challenges in international organizations. He emphasized the critical role of information security in protecting sensitive development data and financial systems, highlighting the importance of robust security measures in today's digital landscape.
