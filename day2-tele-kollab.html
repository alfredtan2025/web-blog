DAY 2 JOURNEY

Join us as we explore and discover our incredible Day 1 journey through two remarkable companies.

By Alfred Tan
June 10, 2025

Teleperformance PH

SM Aura Office, Bonifacio Global City (BGC), Taguig City

Ms. Beatrice Grace Bonjibod, Brand Marketing Specialist, introduced TP as a global leader in digitally integrated business services beyond traditional voice support, highlighting its focus on innovation and human-centric solutions.

She also emphasized TP's commitment to diversity, noting that 53% of their workforce are women, and encouraged students to build soft skills like communication and adaptability alongside technical skills.

We toured TP's Innovation and Solutions Hub, seeing firsthand how automation, analytics, and digital platforms drive customer experience. Also joined an interactive session with the HR team to learn about internships, recruitment, and including mock interviews.

Kollab

Greenfield Tower, Mandaluyong City

Jazmine Calma, Senior QA Engineer, talked about the importance of healthy and inclusive workplace culture in achieving product excellence and personal growth.

Toni-Jan Keith Monserrat, Senior Principal Engineer for AI and Data, presented practical applications of AI in real-world industries like agriculture, health, and customer service, inspiring students to think beyond theory.

Miguel Siriban, UI/UX Designer and Developer, gave practical advice on building a strong development portfolio to stand out to employers.
